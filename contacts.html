My first cartoon movie

SpongeBob SquarePants

SpongeBob SquarePants is an American animated television series created by marine science educator and animator Stephen Hillenburg that premiered on Nickelodeon as a sneak peek after the 1999 Kids' Choice Awards on May 1, 1999, and officially premiered on July 17, 1999.

It chronicles the adventures of the title character and his aquatic friends in the underwater city of Bikini Bottom

Interesting Facts

  1. Patrick and Gary are related
  2. Bikini Bottom exists
  3. SpongeBob is over 35 years old
  • Patrick weighs only 56 grams
  • The creator of the series is a marine biologist
  • SpongeBob's laugh is based on the sound of a dolphin
